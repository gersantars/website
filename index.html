2020-2021 Ecosistemas Minerales + Silicon // Ecosystem // Matter

From the dust in our flesh come minerals that return to the earth. The layers settle and return to our hands transformed into technological matter. What if what moves us is nothing more than the collaboration of these agents in deep strata that pulsate under our skin?

2019 Orquidarium + Orchids // Nature // Beauty

The world of orchids becomes a case study to discuss issues such as conservation, beauty and anthropocentrism. Orchids have been objects of contemplation, consumption and reproduction since the beginning of European colonialism and finally become an exotic object that has been brought to other territories to be domesticated.

2019 Fragile + Scenography // Love //Fiction

Fragile focuses on human interrelationships of a romantic nature and the fiction created from them with an eagerness to tear apart appearances. In this case, the act of revealing the mythical aspect of love appears in the production of three models of an intimate domestic space.
